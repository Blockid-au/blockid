# 2. Core Problem

SMEs and startups today suffer from fragmented identity. Private companies operate using disconnected systems: spreadsheets, PDFs, email, accounting software, legal documents, manual governance, and disconnected investor records.

This creates: unclear ownership, untrusted valuations, poor governance, difficult fundraising, weak due diligence, low investor confidence, poor liquidity.

Investor trust is the biggest problem. Investors do not trust: revenue claims, valuation claims, ownership records, governance quality, operational consistency.

The market lacks:

# "A persistent trust identity for private companies."

# 5. Core Platform Architecture

- **Layer 1 — Business Identity Layer:** Persistent BlockID containing legal identity, registration, ABN/ASIC data, founders, ownership graph, governance graph, trust score, valuation history, funding history.
- **Layer 2 — AI Intelligence Layer:** AI agents analyze financial health, valuation, governance, funding readiness, investor risk, ownership clarity, operational maturity.
- **Layer 3 — Blockchain Trust Layer:** Stores ownership proofs, valuation snapshots, governance approvals, fundraising events, share issuance, transfer history, trust anchors. Acts as **immutable trust infrastructure.**
- **Layer 4 — Marketplace Layer:** Companies can list privately, raise capital, showcase equity opportunities, connect with investors, exchange strategic equity, access secondary liquidity.

# 6. Product Modules

### Module 1 — Persistent BlockID
Identity profile, business timeline (rounds, governance, valuation, milestones), trust profile (trust score, governance score, operational consistency, reporting quality, funding readiness).

### Module 2 — AI Cap Table OS
Founder/preferred/ordinary shares, vesting, ESOP, SAFE/convertible notes; AI dilution simulation; AI strategic advisory on funding strategy, cap structure, founder control, employee allocation.

### Module 3 — AI Valuation Engine *(core differentiator)*
Inputs: accounting, bank feeds, invoices, Stripe, payroll, contracts, subscriptions, growth, retention, cash flow, burn, industry benchmarks.
Outputs: market-based, revenue-multiple, risk-adjusted valuation; funding readiness score; investor confidence score. AI comparison engine vs similar startups, regional data, sector multiples, growth-stage benchmarks. Result: **Verified SME Valuation.**

### Module 4 — Investor Trust Layer
**BlockID Trust Score™** combining financial quality, governance, ownership clarity, operational maturity, reporting consistency, founder reliability, legal hygiene. Investor dashboard with valuation history, governance, cap table, trust score, financials, fundraising.

### Module 5 — AI Due Diligence Room
AI document analysis (financials, shareholder agreements, contracts, payroll, tax, investor docs); AI risk summary (missing docs, governance, financial inconsistencies, ownership conflicts, compliance); investor-ready data room auto-generated.

### Module 6 — Governance Infrastructure
Board management (resolutions, approvals, meetings, voting, shareholder notices). Blockchain governance proofs for approvals, resolutions, voting, ownership changes.

### Module 7 — Marketplace Infrastructure *(NOT phase 1)*
Launches only after trust infrastructure exists. Private listings, equity showcase (equity, SAFE, convertibles, partnerships), AI investor matching, future secondary marketplace for private share transfers and liquidity.

# 7. Blockchain Strategy

**Principle:** Blockchain is NOT the product — it is **trust infrastructure.**

**Architecture:** SaaS-first, blockchain-backed.

- **BlockID Main Trust Network** — stores trust anchors, valuation snapshots, governance proofs, ownership proofs, identity references.
- **Enterprise Chains (optional per company)** — governance, cap table, operational and ownership records.

**Stack:** Cosmos SDK, Tendermint, permissioned sovereign chains. Reasons: sovereign architecture, modular, interoperable, enterprise governance, permission control, multi-chain scalability.

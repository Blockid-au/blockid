## 4. Unit Economics & Cohort Analysis

### 4.1 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**CAC Calculation:**
```
CAC = (Sales & Marketing Spend) ÷ (New Customers Acquired)
```

**Year 1 CAC Assumption:** A$400 per customer
- S&M spend: A$18K
- New customers: 45
- CAC: A$18K ÷ 45 = A$400

**CAC by Channel:**
| Channel | Cost/Customer | Volume | Total |
|---------|---------------|--------|-------|
| Organic (SEO + referral) | 50 | 25 customers | 50% |
| Paid ads (Google, LinkedIn) | 200 | 15 customers | 33% |
| Partnerships/Affiliates | 300 | 5 customers | 11% |
| **Blended CAC** | **A$400** | **45 total** | **100%** |

**Target:** CAC declines to A$250 by Year 3 as brand strength and referral grow

### 4.2 Lifetime Value (LTV)

**LTV Calculation:**
```
LTV = (ARPU) × (Gross Margin %) × (12 ÷ Monthly Churn %)
LTV = (ARPU) × (Gross Margin %) × (Average Customer Lifespan in months)
```

**Year 1 LTV Assumption:**
- ARPU: A$1,250/year = A$104/month
- Gross Margin: 85%
- Monthly Churn: 8%
- Average lifespan: 12 ÷ 0.08 = 150 months ≈ 12.5 years
- **LTV = A$104 × 0.85 × 150 = A$13,260**

**Conservative LTV (capped at 48 months for SaaS):**
- LTV = A$104 × 0.85 × 48 = A$4,262 ≈ A$4,000

**LTV:CAC Ratio:** A$4,000 ÷ A$400 = 10:1 (excellent, indicates sustainable unit economics)

**Target LTV by Year:**
- Year 1: A$4,000 (payback = 14 months)
- Year 2: A$5,000 (payback = 12 months, lower churn)
- Year 3: A$6,000 (payback = 9 months, strong retention)

### 6.2 Valuation by Method

**Method 1: Revenue Multiple (Current)**
```
Year 1 ARR: A$250K
Multiple: 8x (early-stage, pre-revenue SaaS)
Valuation: A$250K × 8 = A$2M
```

**Method 2: VC Method (Forward-Looking)**
```
Exit value assumption: A$50M (10-year goal, conservative vs. Carta at A$200M+)
Target return for seed investor: 25x
Required post-money at seed: A$50M ÷ 25 = A$2M
Investor dilution: 20%
Pre-money valuation: A$2M × (80% ÷ 20%) = A$8M
→ Seed valuation: A$2M post-money
→ Pre-seed valuation (before Series A): A$440K pre-money
```

**Method 3: Future Revenue Valuation (Discounted)**
```
Year 2 ARR projection: A$1.05M
Forward multiple: 10x = A$10.5M post-Series A
Discount for time (pre-seed is 1 year earlier): 40%
Current pre-money: A$10.5M × 40% = A$4.2M (too high for pre-seed)

Alternative: Use Year 1 revenue with discount
Year 1 ARR: A$250K, Multiple 6x = A$1.5M
```

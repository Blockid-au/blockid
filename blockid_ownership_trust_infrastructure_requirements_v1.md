# BlockID.au — Ownership & Trust Infrastructure Requirements

## Product Direction & Execution Brief for Team

---

# 1. CORE VISION

BlockID.au is building:

> **The trust layer for startup ownership and fundraising.**

The platform must help:

* founders
* co-founders
* investors
* advisors

not only prove ownership,
but also:

* manage ownership
* understand ownership value
* track growth
* monitor equity performance
* increase investor confidence
* reduce fundraising friction

---

# 2. CORE PROBLEM

Current startup ownership management is fragmented across:

* spreadsheets
* PDFs
* emails
* disconnected cap table tools
* manual agreements

This creates:

* unclear ownership
* low transparency
* low investor trust
* difficult due diligence
* poor visibility into equity value
* founder disputes
* inability for investors/co-founders to understand real asset value

---

# 3. CORE PRODUCT PRINCIPLE

Ownership must become:

* trusted
* verifiable
* trackable
* understandable
* valuable
* transparent

The platform must make ownership feel like:

> a real, visible, manageable digital asset.

---

# 4. PRIMARY PRODUCT POSITIONING

DO NOT position as:

* blockchain product
* crypto platform
* token trading system

Position as:

> Ownership & Fundraising Intelligence Platform

Or:

> Trusted Ownership Infrastructure for Startups

---

# 5. CORE USER BENEFIT

Users must immediately feel:

### "I actually own something valuable and visible."

not:

> "I just have shares written somewhere."

---

# 6. REQUIRED USER EXPERIENCE

The platform must allow:

* founders
* co-founders
* investors

to clearly see:

* what they own
* ownership percentage
* ownership history
* ownership changes
* ownership dilution
* current valuation estimate
* projected future value
* growth of their asset over time

---

# 7. CORE FEATURE REQUIREMENTS

# A. TRUSTED OWNERSHIP IDENTITY

Every startup must have:

* unique company identity
* unique ownership registry
* verified ownership records
* tamper-evident ownership history

---

## Requirements

* create startup identity from idea stage
* unique startup ownership profile
* unique shareholder IDs
* ownership timestamping
* ownership proof records
* immutable ownership history logs

---

* manage shareholder records

* ownership %

* ownership changes

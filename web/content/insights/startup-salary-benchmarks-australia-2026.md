## Understanding 2026 Australian Startup Salary Benchmarks

As an Australian startup founder, ensuring your team is fairly compensated is crucial for attracting and retaining top talent. However, determining competitive salaries can be challenging, especially in a rapidly evolving market. In this guide, we'll provide you with 2026 Australian startup salary benchmarks, covering key roles and stages.

### Key Roles and Salary Ranges

Here's a breakdown of average salary ranges for key roles in Australian startups:

| Role | Pre-Seed | Seed | Series A | Series B+ |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Software Engineer | $90,000 - $120,000 | $100,000 - $130,000 | $120,000 - $150,000 | $140,000 - $160,000 |
| Product Manager | $100,000 - $130,000 | $110,000 - $140,000 | $130,000 - $150,000 | $150,000 - $170,000 |
| Growth Marketer | $70,000 - $100,000 | $80,000 - $110,000 | $100,000 - $120,000 | $120,000 - $140,000 |
| Sales Representative | $60,000 - $90,000 + commission | $70,000 - $100,000 + commission | $80,000 - $110,000 + commission | $100,000 - $130,000 + commission |
| CTO | $120,000 - $160,000 | $140,000 - $180,000 | $160,000 - $200,000 | $180,000 - $220,000 |

### Equity Offsets for Below-Market Salaries

> "Startups often use equity to offset below-market salaries. However, it's essential to strike a balance between cash and equity compensation to avoid over-diluting founder ownership." - [Australian Startup Genome Report](https://www.startupgenome.com/research/australian-startup-ecosystem)

When offering below-market salaries, consider providing equity to make up for the difference. This approach can help attract top talent while preserving cash flow.

### Contractor vs Employee Considerations

When hiring, you'll need to decide between contractors and employees. Here's a comparison:

|  | Contractors | Employees |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Tax Obligations** | Contractor responsible | Employer responsible |
| **Superannuation** | Not required | Required (10.5% - 12%) |
| **Leave Entitlements** | Not entitled | Entitled (annual, sick, carer's) |
| **Termination** | Easier termination | More complex termination |

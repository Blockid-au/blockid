## Australian Government Grants for Startups 2026: Non-Dilutive Funding

As an Australian startup founder, you're likely no stranger to the challenges of securing funding. While equity investment can be a great way to grow your business, it's not always the best option. That's where government grants come in – a non-dilutive funding source that doesn't require giving up equity. In this guide, we'll explore the Australian government grants available for startups in 2026, including eligibility, application tips, and timelines.

### Overview of Australian Government Grants for Startups

The Australian government offers various grants to support startups at different stages of growth. These grants are designed to help entrepreneurs turn their ideas into successful businesses. Here are some of the most notable grants:

| Grant Program | Funding Amount | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Entrepreneurs Programme (Growth Stream) | Up to $1M | Supports high-growth startups with innovative products or services |
| CRC-P Grants | Up to $3M | Encourages collaboration between industry, researchers, and startups |
| Accelerating Commercialisation | Up to $1.1M | Helps startups commercialize their products or services |
| MTP Connect | Up to $1M | Supports medical technology and pharmaceutical startups |

### Entrepreneurs Programme (Growth Stream)

The Entrepreneurs Programme is a key initiative of the Australian government to support startups. The Growth Stream provides funding of up to $1M to support high-growth startups with innovative products or services.

> **"The Entrepreneurs Programme is designed to help startups grow and succeed, with a focus on innovative and scalable businesses."** [Australian Government Website](https://www.entrepreneurs.gov.au/)

To be eligible for the Growth Stream, your startup must:

* Be an Australian-based business
* Have a turnover of less than $5M
* Have a plan for growth and expansion
* Be able to demonstrate the potential for significant economic benefits

### CRC-P Grants

The Cooperative Research Centres Programme (CRC-P) provides funding to encourage collaboration between industry, researchers, and startups. The grants are worth up to $3M and are designed to support projects that have the potential to generate significant economic benefits.

### Accelerating Commercialisation

The Accelerating Commercialisation program provides funding of up to $1.1M to help startups commercialize their products or services. The program is designed to support startups that have a strong potential for growth and scalability.

### MTP Connect

MTP Connect is a government-backed initiative that provides funding to medical technology and pharmaceutical startups. The program offers grants of up to $1M to support startups in the early stages of commercialization.

### State-Based Grants

In addition to the federal government grants, there are also state-based grants available to startups. Some of the notable state-based grants include:

* NSW MVP Fund: Provides funding of up to $500,000 to support startups in New South Wales
* Vic LaunchVic: Offers funding of up to $1M to support startups in Victoria

### Eligibility and Application Tips

To be eligible for government grants, your startup must meet the specific criteria for each program. Here are some general tips for applying:

* Ensure your startup meets the eligibility criteria
* Develop a strong business plan and pitch
* Provide detailed financial projections
* Demonstrate the potential for significant economic benefits

## Effective Investor Updates in Australia: Templates & Best Practices

Sending regular investor updates is crucial for maintaining a strong relationship with your investors and keeping them informed about your startup's progress. However, many founders struggle with crafting effective updates that provide value to their investors. In fact, **80% of founders send bad investor updates**, often too long, lacking clear asks, and focused on vanity metrics.

### The Importance of Investor Updates

Investor updates serve several purposes:

* Keep investors informed about your startup's progress and milestones
* Demonstrate your ability to execute and make data-driven decisions
* Show transparency and accountability
* Provide an opportunity to make asks and seek guidance or support

## What Makes a Good Investor Update?

A good investor update should be concise, focused on key metrics, and include a clear ask. Here's a template you can use:

### Investor Update Template

| **Category** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| **Wins** | 3 key achievements or milestones |
| **Challenges** | 3 current challenges or areas for improvement |
| **Ask** | 1 specific ask or request for guidance/support |
| **Key Metrics** | Table with MRR, Runway, Users, and other relevant metrics |

### Example Investor Update

* **Wins:**
	+ Signed 5 new customers in the past month
	+ Increased MRR by 20%
	+ Launched a new feature
* **Challenges:**
	+ Difficulty in scaling our sales team
	+ High customer acquisition costs
	+ Limited resources for marketing
* **Ask:**
	+ Seeking guidance on how to optimize our sales process
* **Key Metrics:**
	| Metric | Value |
	| --- | --- |
	| MRR | $10,000 |
	| Runway | 12 months |
	| Users | 1,000 |

## Best Practices for Investor Updates

* **Keep it concise**: Aim for a length of 1-2 pages at most
* **Focus on key metrics**: Include MRR, Runway, Users, and other relevant metrics
* **Make a clear ask**: Specify what you need help with or guidance on
* **Use a consistent cadence**: Send updates monthly for active investors and quarterly for angel investors

## How Often to Send Investor Updates

The frequency of investor updates depends on the type of investor and the stage of your startup. Here are some guidelines:

* **Active investors**: Monthly updates
* **Angel investors**: Quarterly updates
* **VC investors**: Monthly or bi-monthly updates

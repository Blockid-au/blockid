## Building in Public Australia: Boost Credibility & Raise Capital

As an Australian founder, you're likely no stranger to the challenges of building a successful startup. But have you considered the power of building in public? By sharing your metrics, milestones, and even failures on social media platforms like Twitter, LinkedIn, and Reddit, you can boost your credibility with investors and customers, and ultimately raise capital.

### What is Building in Public?

Building in public is a marketing strategy that involves sharing your startup's progress, successes, and challenges with a wider audience. This can include metrics such as revenue growth, user acquisition numbers, and customer satisfaction scores, as well as milestones like product launches and funding rounds.

## Benefits of Building in Public

Building in public can have numerous benefits for Australian founders. Here are just a few:

### Community Building

Building in public can also help you build a community of supporters and advocates for your startup. By sharing your journey and engaging with your audience, you can create a loyal following that can provide valuable feedback and support.

### Improved Marketing

Building in public can also be a effective marketing strategy. By sharing your startup's story and progress, you can create a compelling narrative that resonates with your target audience.

## What to Share vs What to Keep Private

While building in public can be beneficial, it's essential to strike the right balance between sharing information and keeping sensitive data private. Here are some guidelines on what to share and what to keep private:

### Share:

* **Metrics**: Revenue growth, user acquisition numbers, customer satisfaction scores
* **Milestones**: Product launches, funding rounds, major partnerships
* **Lessons learned**: Share your failures and what you learned from them

### Keep Private:

* **Sensitive financial information**: Avoid sharing detailed financial information or projections
* **Competitive data**: Avoid sharing data that could give your competitors an advantage

## Best Practices for Building in Public

Here are some best practices to keep in mind when building in public:

### Be Consistent

* **Post regularly**: Share updates on a regular basis to keep your audience engaged
* **Use a consistent tone**: Use a consistent tone and voice across all your social media platforms

### Be Authentic

* **Be honest**: Share your successes and failures honestly and transparently
* **Be vulnerable**: Share your challenges and vulnerabilities to build trust with your audience

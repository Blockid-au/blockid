### What problem are you solving and for whom?

**The problem: Founders build in the dark.**

Ninety percent of startups fail (Failory 2026). In Australia, it is worse -- 60% die within three years (ABS June 2025) and 75% are gone within five years (Inside Small Business). In FY2024-25 alone, 437,150 new Australian businesses started while 370,500 closed -- a 30.3% annual churn rate.

The hidden cause embedded in 70% of "ran out of cash" failures (CB Insights) is a valuation black hole. Founders cannot quantify their company's worth. They make equity decisions blindly. They pay A$5,000 to A$50,000 for a manual valuation that takes 2-6 weeks -- by which time critical decisions have already been made with bad data or no data at all.

The AI explosion has made this worse. $97 billion flowed into AI startup funding in 2024 (34% of all VC -- Second Talent), and $252.3 billion in total corporate AI investment (Stanford HAI 2025). Yet 90% of AI startups fail within 18 months (AI4SP). AI generates ideas faster than ever, but it cannot build businesses. The gap between "AI-generated idea" and "sustainable company" is wider than ever.

**Who we serve:**
- Early-stage founders (pre-revenue to Series A) who need to understand, prove, and grow their startup's value
- Founders preparing for fundraise who need data room materials, valuation intelligence, and equity modelling
- Accelerators and VCs who want portfolio-level analytics and standardised scoring across their cohorts
- The 2,600+ active startups in Australia (Startup Genome), served by 300+ accelerators and 15,000+ angel investors (AAAI) -- none of whom have a unified platform for the full startup lifecycle

### What is your solution?

BlockID.au is an AI-powered platform that gives founders instant intelligence about their startup's value -- and then grows with them from idea to exit.

**What is live today (Phase 1 -- COMPLETE):**

1. **Startup Value Index (SVI) Analysis:** AI generates a comprehensive 10-page R&D report in 60 seconds, scoring the startup across 8 dimensions -- Team, Market, Product, Traction, Cap Table, Investor Readiness, Legal, and Moat. Three parallel AI batches run concurrently to deliver speed.

2. **10 Free Tools:** Idea Valuation, Equity Split Calculator, Dilution Modeler, Cap Table Diff, Funding Plan Generator, Data Room Checklist, Term Sheet Analyzer, Cofounder Match, R&D Tax Calculator, and ESIC Eligibility Checker. These tools drive organic traffic and convert users to the core SVI product.

3. **Multi-model AI Engine:** Unified client that chains Claude (Anthropic), OpenAI GPT-4o-mini, Google Gemini 2.0 Flash, and proxy fallback -- with automatic failover, budget tracking, and cost optimisation. If one provider fails, the system silently falls through to the next.

**What is in progress (Phase 2 -- Q3 2026):**

4. **Evidence Vault:** Founders upload documents, connect data sources (GitHub, Analytics, Stripe), and their SVI score updates in real-time. The report becomes a living document that grows with the startup.

5. **Milestone Badges & Weekly Reports:** Automated progress tracking with AI-generated weekly email summaries.

**What comes next (Phases 3-8):**

6. Dollar valuation engine with multiples, comparables, and DCF (Q4 2026)
7. Full cap table management with vesting, ESOP, and share classes (Q1 2027)
8. Blockchain equity tokenization on a private Cosmos SDK chain with MetaMask integration (Q2-Q3 2027)
9. Investment tools: one-click data room, fundraise wizard, investor CRM (Q3-Q4 2027)
10. Revenue dashboards, on-chain dividend distribution, and exit modelling (2028+)

**One platform. Entire startup lifecycle. From idea to IPO.**

### Why now?

The timing for BlockID is driven by three converging forces:

**1. The AI idea explosion demands validation infrastructure.**
$252.3 billion was invested in AI in 2024 (Stanford HAI), yet 90% of AI startups fail. AI is generating ideas at unprecedented scale, but the tools to validate, score, and track those ideas from concept to viable business do not exist. BlockID fills this gap.

**2. Australian regulatory and ecosystem tailwinds.**
Australia's startup ecosystem is maturing with 2,600+ active startups, A$15 billion in ecosystem funding, and growing government support (R&D Tax Incentive, ESIC concessions). But the infrastructure -- valuation, equity management, compliance -- is still manual, fragmented, and expensive. The market is ready for a unified platform.

**3. Blockchain equity tokenization is becoming technically feasible.**
Cosmos SDK, CosmWasm smart contracts, and MetaMask integration via Ethermint have matured to the point where equity tokenization on a private chain is achievable for a startup (our Phase 5, targeted Q2-Q3 2027). This was not possible two years ago. First movers who combine AI valuation with blockchain equity will own the category.

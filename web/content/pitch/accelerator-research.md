## 1. Startmate Accelerator

**Website:** [startmate.com/accelerator/program](https://www.startmate.com/accelerator/program)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Next Application Deadline** | **17 May 2026** (just passed — check for late applications or next intake) |
| **Program Dates** | 27 July -- 16 October 2026 (12 weeks) |
| **Format** | Hybrid: 3 in-person weeks in Melbourne (Week 0, Week 7, Weeks 11-12 Demo Day), remainder remote |
| **Investment** | A$120,000 on a $1.5M post-money SAFE (first-time fundraisers) |
| **For Previously Funded** | Matching valuation from previous round (min $250K AUD raised) |

### What They Offer
- A$120K investment per startup
- Direct mentor access (mentors invest personally in the fund)
- Network of 4,000+ ANZ founders, operators, and investors
- Dedicated fundraising support and investor introductions
- Talent access through Startmate fellowships
- Select cohort members receive San Francisco investor meetings
- Demo Day with 1,000+ attendees

### Requirements
- Founders at any stage (67% of Accelerator companies had no revenue when they applied)
- Australian or New Zealand based
- Full-time commitment during the 12-week program

### How to Apply
- Apply at [universe.startmate.com](https://universe.startmate.com/sign-up?next-page=/apply&program=recRX9IODNkDTWDQs)
- Two-stage evaluation: online application with 90-second video, then in-depth interviews with mentors

### BlockID Fit Assessment
- **HIGH FIT** -- Startmate is the top AU accelerator for early-stage SaaS. BlockID is live with real product, AI-powered, and solving a real problem. The $120K + network access is extremely valuable. The 17 May deadline just passed; monitor for next cohort or contact directly for late consideration.

## 2. Antler Australia

**Website:** [antler.co/apply](https://www.antler.co/apply)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Next Application Deadline** | **Rolling applications** (no fixed deadline) |
| **Cohort Start Date** | 27 July 2026 |
| **Locations** | Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane |
| **Selectivity** | Less than 2% of applicants accepted |
| **Investment** | Pre-seed investment; up to $30M available through Series C via Antler Elevate |

### What They Offer
- Pre-seed inception-stage investment
- Long-term partnership ("not a programme you pass through")
- Access to Antler's global platform across 26 locations worldwide
- Growth-stage capital available through Antler Elevate (Series A onwards)
- Mentorship and co-founder matching (no prior idea required, though most have one)

### Requirements
- Ambition, domain expertise, and drive to build something significant
- Full-time commitment (100% focus required)
- Necessary visa documentation for Australia
- No prior business idea required, though most applicants have direction

### How to Apply
- Complete online application at [antler.co/apply](https://www.antler.co/apply)
- Fields: name, email, LinkedIn (optional), preferred location
- Applications are accepted on a rolling basis

### BlockID Fit Assessment
- **MEDIUM-HIGH FIT** -- Antler invests at Day 0, but BlockID already has a live product, which positions it well. The global network across 26 locations is valuable for expansion. Apply immediately since rolling applications for the July 2026 cohort are open now.

## 3. Google for Startups Accelerator: Australia

**Website:** [startup.google.com/programs/accelerator/australia](https://startup.google.com/programs/accelerator/australia/)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Next Application Deadline** | **Applications currently closed** -- register interest for future cohorts |
| **Program Duration** | 10 weeks (hybrid) |
| **Format** | Mix of remote and in-person sessions in Sydney |
| **Cohort Size** | 10-15 startups |
| **Investment** | **Equity-free** (no equity taken) |

### What They Offer
- Completely equity-free program
- Dedicated mentoring from Google teams and industry experts
- Technical support on AI/ML challenges through 1-to-1 sessions
- Access to early Google AI products via Trusted Tester and Early Access Program
- Google Cloud credits (subject to eligibility)
- Free Cloud TPU access for machine learning research
- Workshops on product design, customer acquisition, and leadership
- Demo Day event to pitch to investors and Alphabet leaders

### Requirements
- Australian-based startups
- Building AI/ML-driven platforms and products
- Seed to Series A (or equivalent) maturity
- Demonstrating meaningful traction
- CTO and technical leadership must commit to full program participation
- **Not eligible:** Non-profits, government agencies, businesses with existing Google employees

### How to Apply
- Register interest at [startup.google.com/programs/accelerator/australia](https://startup.google.com/programs/accelerator/australia/)
- Previous cohorts ran September-November; next 2026 dates TBA
- Also consider the AI First track: [startup.google.com/programs/accelerator/ai-first/australia](https://startup.google.com/programs/accelerator/ai-first/australia/)

### BlockID Fit Assessment
- **HIGH FIT** -- BlockID uses AI/ML for valuation scoring, making it a strong candidate for both the Australia and AI First programs. Equity-free is ideal. Register interest immediately and monitor for the next cohort opening.

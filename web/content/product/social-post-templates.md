## Usage Notes

### Formatting Tips
- LinkedIn truncates posts after approximately 210 characters. Place the hook in the first line so it appears before "...see more".
- Use line breaks generously. Short paragraphs perform better on LinkedIn.
- Avoid emojis in the hook line. Use them sparingly (if at all) in the body.
- Always place hashtags at the end, never inline.

### Customisation Guide
- Replace placeholder values in curly braces `{}` with real content before posting.
- Adjust statistics as real data becomes available from the platform.
- For Template 3 (social proof), update milestone numbers as they grow (100th, 500th, 1000th).
- For Template 5 (article share), swap the example article with the actual article being shared.

### Posting Cadence
- Rotate through templates across the week.
- Do not use the same template type two days in a row.
- Template 2 (educational) and Template 5 (article share) perform best on Tuesday-Thursday.
- Template 4 (tool promotion) works well on Mondays and Fridays when founders are planning their week.

### UTM Parameters
Append these to all URLs for tracking:
- LinkedIn: `?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign={template-type}`
- Twitter/X: `?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign={template-type}`

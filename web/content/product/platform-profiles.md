## 300-Word (Medium Profile)
BlockID.au is the agentic AI valuation platform that helps founders build investable businesses from day one. Whether you're at the idea stage or preparing for your first fundraise, BlockID gives you the tools, intelligence, and structure to prove your startup's value.

**The Startup Value Index (SVI)**
Our core product analyses your startup across 8 evidence-backed dimensions: Founder & Team Value, Market & Problem Clarity, Product & Technical Depth, Traction & Revenue Evidence, Cap Table & Governance Health, Investor Readiness Index, Legal & Compliance, and Strategic Vision & Moat. In under 60 seconds, you get a comprehensive score (30-300), personalised action plan, and an 11-page PDF report with specific next steps.

**Free Tools for Every Founder**
We believe every founder deserves access to professional-grade tools: equity split calculator, dilution modelling, cap table management, term sheet AI analysis, funding plan builder, data room checklist, and co-founder matching. No credit card required.

**Workspace & Dashboard**
Paid users unlock a complete startup workspace: Evidence Vault for uploading proof documents, real-time SVI tracking with weekly trend charts, compliance checker across 13 Australian regulatory requirements, estimated valuation ranges, industry benchmarks, priority task dashboard, and auto-generated data room templates ready for investors.

**AI-Powered Intelligence**
BlockID uses advanced AI (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini) to generate personalised reports, competitive research, pitch deck outlines, and weekly insights emails. Every analysis is mentor-quality — warm, encouraging, and practically useful.

**Built for Australia**
Native support for ASIC compliance, ESIC eligibility, R&D Tax Incentive, Australian cap table structures, and AUD pricing. Built in Sydney by Auschain PTY LTD.

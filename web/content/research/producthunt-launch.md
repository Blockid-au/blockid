## Launch Timing

### Best Day
**Tuesday** -- consistently the highest traffic day on ProductHunt. Avoid Mondays (competition with weekend backlog) and Fridays (lower engagement).

### Best Time
**12:01 AM PT (5:01 PM AEST)** -- ProductHunt resets daily at midnight Pacific Time. Launching at 12:01 AM PT gives the full 24-hour cycle. This is 5:01 PM AEST, which means the Australian founder community can upvote during their evening, and the US/global audience sees it when they wake up.

### Recommended Launch Date
Target a Tuesday when there are no major tech events, Apple keynotes, or other high-profile launches competing for attention. Avoid the first week of a month (busy with reporting) and holiday weeks.

### Upvote Etiquette
- **Never** ask people to "upvote" directly in a public post -- ProductHunt may penalise this.
- **Do** say: "We just launched on ProductHunt -- would love your feedback" and share the link.
- **Do** ask supporters to leave a genuine comment (comments boost ranking more than upvotes alone).
- **Do** ask people to try the product, not just vote. Authentic engagement matters.
- **Never** use upvote exchange groups or services. ProductHunt actively detects and penalises this.

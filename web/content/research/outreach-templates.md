## Personalisation Notes

- **Always personalise the first line.** Reference something specific: a LinkedIn post, a company milestone, a mutual connection, or an event they attended. Generic openers get ignored.
- **Never attach files in cold outreach.** Link to the platform. Let them explore on their own terms.
- **Follow up once, maximum twice.** If no response after two touches, move on. Persistence crosses into annoyance quickly.
- **Track everything.** Use a CRM (even a spreadsheet) to log sends, opens, replies, and conversions. Review weekly.
- **A/B test subject lines.** Rotate between question-based ("Have you valued your startup yet?") and statement-based ("Your startup valuation in 60 seconds") to find what resonates.

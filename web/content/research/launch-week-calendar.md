## Day 6 (Saturday): Industry Insight

**Post Time:** 10:00 AM AEST

**Content:**

$252 billion was invested in AI in 2024. And 90% of AI startups will fail.

Let that sink in for a moment.

The Stanford HAI 2025 report shows corporate AI investment hit $252.3 billion last year. AI startup funding alone was $97 billion -- 34% of all global venture capital.

But the McKinsey 2025 State of AI report tells the other side: only 33% of companies scale AI beyond pilot. MIT and Capgemini found that 88-95% of AI pilots fail to lift financials. And 60%+ of AI tools have no recurring revenue model.

We are living through the biggest investment boom in AI history. And also the biggest gap between money in and value out.

The problem is not technology. Anyone can build an AI product in a weekend. The problem is business viability. Most AI founders can demonstrate a product but cannot demonstrate a business.

That gap -- between "I built an AI tool" and "I have a sustainable AI company" -- is what BlockID.au is designed to close.

Our Startup Value Index measures the things that matter beyond the demo: market fit, revenue model, team readiness, investor documentation, and competitive defensibility.

Because the founders who survive the next 18 months will not be the ones with the best AI. They will be the ones who can prove their business works.

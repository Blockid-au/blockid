## Production Notes

- Tone: Confident, passionate, conversational -- like a real founder who has lived this problem
- Pacing: 140-160 WPM overall. Slow down for key stats. Speed up for feature lists.
- The bold emphasis words should rise in pitch approximately 20-30% above baseline
- Italic sections should be delivered 10-15% faster than normal pace
- Pauses are critical -- they let the audience absorb shocking stats before moving on
- Rising intonation on questions, setups, and anticipation
- Falling intonation on declarations, finality, and punchlines
- The "Only us" repetition in Scene 9 should build in confidence each time -- louder, more emphatic
- Scene 11 (Drop Mic) should be the slowest, most deliberate section -- every word lands
- Voice model: ElevenLabs multilingual_v2, Australian English, male

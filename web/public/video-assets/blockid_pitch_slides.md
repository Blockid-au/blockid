## Design Guidelines

- **Primary Background:** #0B1220 (dark navy)
- **Brand Color:** #3B82F6 (bright blue) for highlights and CTAs
- **Text:** White (#FFFFFF) for headings, #94A3B8 (slate-300) for body
- **Accent Red:** #F87171 for problem/negative stats
- **Accent Green:** #10B981 for solutions/positive stats
- **Accent Gold:** #F59E0B for market/financial stats
- **Font:** Inter or system sans-serif; monospace for metrics
- **Slide ratio:** 16:9 (1920x1080)

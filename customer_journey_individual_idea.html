Why idea-stage is the strategic upstream

  • Top of funnel Captures founders 6–12 months before they would have searched for Carta/Pulley.
  • Sticky Persistent BlockID = lifetime company DNA, started before incorporation.
  • Network Cofounder Match doubles every founder's reach + creates a 2-sided marketplace.
  • Data moat Every idea-valuation feeds AU SME comparables dataset.
  • Conversion Each individual graduates into 1–N early-startup paying customers.

Key moments of truth

  • First idea-valuation result lands in < 60 sec — must "wow"
  • First cofounder profile match notification — magic
  • Equity-split agreement signed by all founders — viral moment
  • Day-0 import: Cap Table v0 → Persistent BlockID without rework

Anti-patterns to avoid

  • Putting idea-stage tools behind signup walls
  • Over-promising valuations — always show range + disclaimer
  • Letting cofounder match become a Tinder of pitches (signal > volume)
  • Forcing equity split decisions before mutual trust is built
  • Charging anything before incorporation — keep idea-stage 100% free

Why early startup is the right wedge

  • Sticky from day 0: persistent BlockID becomes the company's lifetime trust spine.
  • Low CAC via accelerators: one cohort = 20–50 founders onboarded at near-zero cost.
  • Compounding data moat: every early company adds to the AU SME comparable dataset.
  • Each founder graduates into a paying Growth / Series A customer over 12–18 months.

Key moments of truth

  • First Score appears (< 5 min) — must "wow"
  • "Sheets → Cap Table OS" import succeeds without errors
  • First governance event logged (board minute, advisor share)
  • "VC viewed your link 12 min" notification → first paid upgrade
  • Round closes & gets anchored on-chain — trust artifact for next raise

Anti-patterns to avoid

  • Talking blockchain / sovereign chains before talking valuation & cap table
  • Long intake forms · gated downloads · sales-call walls on free tier
  • US-style copy ignoring ASIC / ESIC / R&D context
  • Charging too early — early startups need free until 1st raise
  • Letting BlockID become "set & forget" between rounds

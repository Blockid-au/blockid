# BlockID — Sales-First GTM & Winning Features
## Addendum v1.1 to Master Blueprint

**Operating principle:** Win the first 30 paying customers before building Layer 3 (blockchain), Module 7 (marketplace), or sovereign chains. Every feature in the next 6 months must map to **a buyer ROI in under 30 days.** If a feature does not directly close a deal or retain one, it is deferred.

This addendum sharpens the GTM, narrows the wedge, defines the sales motion, and lists unique features specifically engineered to **close deals**, not impress engineers.

| Filter | Value |

## Tier-3 Enterprise ICP (months 18+)

# 2. Sharpened Wedge — One Product, One Promise

The blueprint says "AI Valuation + Cap Table Infrastructure." That is two products. Pick **one** to lead with.

## The Wedge: Investor-Ready Score™

**One number. One page. One link to share with investors.**

Generated in 5 minutes by connecting Stripe + Xero/MYOB. Rendered as a single shareable PDF + a verified web link with blockchain anchor. Designed to be the artifact a founder sends to a VC the same day.

**Why this wedge wins:**
- Self-serve and instant gratification → low friction to first value
- Designed to be **shared**, which makes every founder a marketer
- Investors who receive it see the full BlockID brand → top-of-funnel for buy-side
- Naturally upsells into Cap Table OS, Data Room, AI Valuation Engine

The Investor-Ready Score is the **wedge artifact**. Everything else is upsell.

# 3. Unique Winning Features (Engineered to Close)

Each feature below has a specific role in moving a deal forward. Order is the order they ship in.

## 3.1 Investor-Ready Score™ *(wedge — week 1)*

A single 0–100 score with five sub-scores: Financials, Cap Table Hygiene, Governance, Founder Background, Documentation. Generated from connected accounting + a 10-question intake. Output: branded PDF + shareable URL + blockchain anchor.

**Sales role:** Free top-of-funnel. Lead magnet that founders generate the same day they engage.

## 3.2 Investor View Link

Magic link that lets founders share their BlockID profile with a specific investor. Tracks who opened it, how long, what sections. Founder gets a notification: "Sequoia partner viewed your data room for 12 minutes."

**Sales role:** Delight moment. Creates retention because the alternative (DocSend) costs USD $300/year. Also: every investor who opens a link is a buy-side lead.

## 3.3 Term Sheet AI

Founder pastes a term sheet. AI returns: a plain-English summary, a founder-friendly redline, comparison to typical AU SAFE/Series A terms, and a dilution simulation tied to their cap table.

**Sales role:** Saves AUD $3k–$10k in lawyer fees. Closes deal in one demo.

## 3.4 Cap Table Diff *(visual before/after)*

Drag in any new round, see exactly who gains, dilutes, and how voting changes. One-click PDF export for the board.

**Sales role:** Replaces 4 hours of spreadsheet work. The "wow" moment in the demo.

## 3.5 Founder Dilution Calculator *(public, ungated, viral)*

Free, no-login web tool. SEO-optimized for "startup dilution calculator Australia." Captures email at the end for a full Investor-Ready Score.

**Sales role:** Top-of-funnel growth loop. Designed to rank #1 on Google AU for the keyword.

## 3.6 One-Click Data Room

Auto-assembled from connected systems. NDA-tracked, watermark-stamped, blockchain-anchored access logs. Investor request → founder click → room ready.

**Sales role:** Replaces DocSend + Notion + Dropbox. Single-vendor consolidation pitch.

## 3.7 Comparable Companies Wall

Show 5 anonymized AU SMEs in the same industry/stage with their valuation multiples. Built from BlockID's proprietary dataset.

**Sales role:** This is the **defensible data moat** and the credibility line in every pitch: *"We have valuation data on 500 AU SMEs that nobody else has."*

## 3.8 Australia-Native Compliance Modules

- **ASIC ABR sync** — auto-pull company registration, directors, share structure
- **ESIC eligibility checker** — flag if the company qualifies for early-stage investor tax incentives (this is a USD $200k–$500k value to an investor)
- **R&D tax incentive readiness** — flag eligible spend
- **AUSTRAC alignment** — for future marketplace

**Sales role:** No US competitor has this. Carta does not. Pulley does not. This is the **AU moat.**

## 3.9 Stripe + Xero/MYOB 5-Minute Plug

OAuth → instant valuation lift. The single most important onboarding step. Time-to-first-value < 5 minutes.

**Sales role:** Removes the #1 reason demos die ("I'll need to send you data later").

## 3.10 White-Label for Accelerators

Co-branded portal for Startmate / Antler / BlueChilli / Stone & Chalk / Cicada cohorts. Every cohort founder gets BlockID, accelerator gets aggregate dashboard.

**Sales role:** One signed accelerator = 20–50 founders onboarded at zero CAC.

## 3.11 "Investor Welcome Pack" *(for VCs)*

Free for VCs. Aggregate dashboard of every BlockID company they've invested in. Quarterly reports auto-generated.

**Sales role:** Investors become passive distribution. They tell every portfolio company: *"Get on BlockID — it's how we want to see updates."*

# 5. Sales Motion — Founder-Led, Then Channel-Led

- Accountants channel activated (Xero ecosystem app listing)
- Lawyers channel (referral fee)
- VCs onboarded as buy-side (free Welcome Pack)
- **Goal:** 500 paying logos, AUD $150k MRR, 5 accelerator partnerships

- Sovereign chain conversations begin

| **"My accountant already does this."** | Your accountant doesn't have AU SME comparable data. We have 500 companies' worth. |

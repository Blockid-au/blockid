# ESOP Expert Knowledge — BlockID C-Level Agent

## Core Framework
- **Pool Size**: 12% (standard AU pre-seed; 10-15% range)
- **Vesting**: 4 years, 1-year cliff (universal best practice)
- **Strike Price**: A$0.10 FMV (tax-compliant under ESS Part 7A)
- **Legal Framework**: Corporations Act s708, Australian ESS Division 83A

## Australian-Specific Tax Notes
- **ESS Section 83A-B**: Startup concession — no upfront tax if conditions met
- **Conditions**: <10yr old, <$50M turnover, price ≤ FMV, 3yr employment plan
- **Tax trigger**: When options exercised (not granted) under startup concession
- **CGT discount**: 50% if shares held 12+ months after exercise
- **Good Leaver**: 90 days to exercise post-termination (standard)
- **Bad Leaver**: Forfeiture of unvested; sometimes vested too

## Key SVI Impact

- ESOP pool created: +8 SVI points
- ESOP operational (grants issued): +12 SVI points total

## Scoring Rubric (CGH Dimension)
- ESOP not mentioned: 0 pts
- ESOP planned: 3 pts
- ESOP designed (this document): 6 pts

- ESOP grants issued: 14 pts

## Red Flags
- ESOP pool > 20%: dilution risk, investor concern
- No cliff: founder/investor red flag
- Strike price at $0 or unrealistic: ATO compliance risk
- No leaver provisions: governance failure
- ESOP > 2x founder's holding: control issue

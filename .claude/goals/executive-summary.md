## Mission
BlockID.au is building Australia's first AI-powered startup valuation and ownership platform — accompanying founders from Day 0 idea through to exit with real-time valuation tracking, transparent equity management, and blockchain-based ownership.

## The Problem
- **60% of Australian startups fail within 3 years** (ABS June 2025). 370,500 businesses closed in FY2024-25.
- **$252.3B invested in AI** in 2024, but **90% of AI startups still fail** with a median lifespan of just 18 months (Stanford HAI 2025, AI4SP).
- **Founders have no affordable way** to track their startup's value, manage equity, or prepare for investors. Manual valuation costs A$5,000-$50,000.

## The Solution
- **AI-Powered Startup Value Index (SVI)**: 10-page analysis in under 60 seconds — scoring across 8 dimensions (team, market, product, traction, cap table, investor readiness, legal, moat).
- **Evidence-Based Growth**: Upload documents to boost your SVI score. Connected sources (Stripe, GitHub, Analytics) increase confidence from 20% to 75%.
- **Full Lifecycle Platform**: From idea validation → equity split → data room → fundraise → tokenized ownership → dividend distribution → exit modeling.

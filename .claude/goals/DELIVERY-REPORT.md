## What BlockID.au Does For You

### Step 1: Know Your Value (60 seconds)
**→ Go to blockid.au, describe your idea, get your Startup Value Index**

You type your idea or upload a business plan. Our AI analyzes it across 8 dimensions:
1. **Founder & Team** — experience, commitment, advisory board
2. **Market & Problem** — TAM/SAM, problem validation, competition
3. **Product & Technical** — MVP readiness, tech stack, demo
4. **Traction & Revenue** — MRR, customers, growth rate
5. **Cap Table & Governance** — equity structure, board, SHA
6. **Investor Readiness** — pitch deck, data room, term sheet understanding
7. **Legal & Compliance** — ASIC, IP, contracts, privacy
8. **Strategic Moat** — competitive advantages, network effects

**You get:** A 10-page report with your SVI score (out of 200), dollar valuation range (Berkus + Scorecard + Revenue Multiple methods), strengths, evidence gaps, and specific action steps.

**Cost:** First analysis free. Then A$1 per report (early-bird).

### Step 2: Prove Your Value (ongoing)
**→ Upload evidence to boost your score**

Connect your real data sources:
- **Upload** pitch deck, financial model, shareholder agreement → score goes up
- **Connect Stripe** → real MRR/ARR auto-imported → score jumps significantly
- **Connect GitHub** → code activity verified → technical score increases
- **Connect Analytics** → real user data → traction score improves

Each piece of verified evidence lifts your SVI score. The more proof you provide, the higher your valuation and the more investor-ready you become.

**You earn badges:** Explorer, Data Driven, Revenue Verified, Investor Ready...

### Step 3: Manage Your Equity (cap table)
**→ Set up your cap table with real share management**

- **Share classes:** Ordinary, Preference A/B, ESOP, Reserved
- **Shareholders:** Add founders, co-founders, investors, advisors
- **Vesting:** 4-year cliff with monthly vesting — fully automated
- **ESOP pool:** Employee share scheme with AU ESS tax compliance
- **Blockchain equity:** Each share = 1 SVT token on BlockID private chain
- **MetaMask:** Shareholders see their shares in their crypto wallet (zero gas fees)

### Step 4: Raise Capital (fundraise)
**→ One-click data room + fundraise wizard**

- **Data room:** Auto-generated from your evidence vault — SVI report, valuation, cap table, metrics all compiled
- **Fundraise rounds:** Configure Pre-Seed, Seed, Series A with SAFE, convertible note, or priced round
- **Dilution modeling:** See exactly how each round affects every shareholder
- **Investor sharing:** Unique link per investor with view tracking (who opened, how long)
- **Term sheet AI:** Upload a term sheet, get red flags highlighted in seconds

### Step 5: Track Revenue & Distribute Dividends
**→ Real-time P&L from Stripe + automated dividend distribution**

- **Revenue dashboard:** MRR, ARR, total revenue, active subscriptions — all from Stripe
- **P&L statement:** Revenue - AI costs - infrastructure = net income
- **Dividends:** Declare dividend, auto-distribute proportional to token holdings
- **AU compliance:** Franking credits calculated automatically (30% corporate rate)
- **Quarterly revaluation:** Updated dollar valuation based on real revenue

### Step 6: Plan Your Exit
**→ Model acquisition, IPO, or secondary sale scenarios**

- **Exit scenarios:** Acquisition at 3x, 5x, 10x, 20x revenue
- **Per-shareholder payout:** Exactly how much each person receives
- **CGT calculation:** Australian 50% discount if shares held >12 months
- **ESOP exercise:** Employee option value at each exit multiple
- **Growth journal:** AI-powered monthly reflections on decisions, pivots, milestones

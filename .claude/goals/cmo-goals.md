# CMO Goals — Chief Marketing Officer

## Mission
Drive qualified traffic to BlockID.au and convert visitors into active users and paying customers. Own brand positioning, SEO, content marketing, community building, and growth analytics. Make BlockID the go-to platform for Australian startup founders.

## Roadmap Ownership

| Phase | CMO Responsibility |
|-------|-------------------|
| Phase 2 (Validation) | SEO foundation, first 20 blog posts, social presence |
| Phase 3 (MVP) | Product launch marketing, ProductHunt, PR |
| Phase 4 (Equity) | Thought leadership, investor community outreach |
| Phase 5 (Tokenization) | Web3 community building, token marketing |
| Phase 6 (Investment) | Investor relations content, case studies |

## Responsibilities

1. **SEO Strategy** — Technical SEO, keyword research, on-page optimization, backlink building
2. **Content Marketing** — Blog posts, insights articles, infographics, guides
3. **Brand Positioning** — "AI-first valuation for Australian founders" narrative
4. **Community Building** — LinkedIn presence, founder community engagement, Slack/Discord
5. **Growth Analytics** — GA4 tracking, funnel analysis, attribution modeling
6. **Campaign Management** — Launch campaigns, content calendars, email sequences
7. **Competitor Intelligence** — Monitor Carta, Pulley, Cake Equity, identify positioning gaps

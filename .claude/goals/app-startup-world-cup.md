## 3-Minute Pitch Script

### Opening Hook (0:00 - 0:20)

> "Last year, 370,500 Australian businesses shut their doors. That is more than one thousand businesses dying every single day. And the number one reason? They ran out of cash -- because they had no idea what their startup was actually worth.
>
> My name is Do Van Long, founder of BlockID.au, and we are fixing this."

### The Problem (0:20 - 0:50)

> "Right now, if you are an early-stage founder and you want to know your startup's value, you have two options. Pay a consultant five to fifty thousand dollars and wait six weeks. Or guess.
>
> Carta, Pulley, Cake Equity -- they all manage cap tables, but none of them tell you what your startup is actually worth. They are spreadsheets with a subscription. And they are all built for Silicon Valley, not for Australian founders dealing with ESIC compliance, ASIC regulations, and the AU startup ecosystem.
>
> The result? 60% of Australian startups fail within three years. Founders fly blind."

### The Solution (0:50 - 1:25)

> "BlockID.au is an AI-powered startup valuation platform. In under 60 seconds, our Startup Value Index analyses your startup across eight dimensions -- team, market, product, traction, cap table, investor readiness, legal compliance, and competitive moat -- and generates a 10-page institutional-grade valuation report.
>
> But we do not stop at a score. Founders connect their evidence -- GitHub commits, Stripe revenue, Google Analytics traffic, LinkedIn profiles -- and our AI watches their startup grow in real time. We call it the Evidence Vault. Your score goes up as your startup proves itself.
>
> We are the platform that takes founders from Day 0 idea all the way through to exit."

### Traction (1:25 - 1:50)

> "BlockID is live today at blockid.au. We have over 50 Australian founders on the platform. We have completed more than 200 SVI analyses. We have 10 free tools live -- equity split calculators, dilution modelling, data room builders, term sheet AI.
>
> Our tech stack runs on six AI providers with automatic fallback -- Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, and more. We auto-publish daily SEO content. We have full analytics tracking with 37 custom GA4 events.
>
> And we have done all of this as a solo technical founder with an AI-augmented team -- seven AI agents operating as my C-suite."

### Market & Business Model (1:50 - 2:20)

> "Australia has 2.7 million active businesses and 437,000 new ones starting every year. The global startup ecosystem is worth 4.4 trillion dollars. Cap table management alone is a 3.2 billion dollar market.
>
> Our model is simple. Free SVI analysis gets founders in the door. One dollar per deep analysis for early adopters. Then 99 to 499 dollars per month for subscriptions. And 20 to 60 thousand dollars per year for accelerator partnerships.
>
> We are Australia-first. ESIC-aware. ASIC-registered. Built by an Australian founder, for Australian founders."

### Close & Ask (2:20 - 3:00)

> "BlockID is not a pitch deck. It is a live product with real users generating real analyses every day. We are raising a pre-seed round of 500 thousand dollars to scale to 500 users, launch cap table management, and begin equity tokenization on Cosmos blockchain.
>
> Our vision? Every startup in Australia knows its value on day one. Every founder has the tools to grow, fundraise, and exit -- without spending fifty thousand dollars on consultants.
>
> BlockID.au -- from idea to exit, one platform.
>
> Thank you."

## Competitive Differentiation Summary

### vs Carta (US, $8.5B valuation)
- Carta is cap table management for post-funding companies ($500+/month)
- No AI valuation, no SVI scoring, no evidence-based growth tracking
- US-centric -- no ESIC, no ASIC compliance, no AU tax guidance
- BlockID targets pre-seed to seed; Carta targets Series A+

### vs Pulley (US, YC-backed)
- Pulley is a simpler cap table tool ($50/month)
- No valuation, no AI analysis, no growth intelligence
- US-focused, 409A valuation only (irrelevant to AU founders)
- BlockID offers free tools as acquisition hook; Pulley is paid-only

### vs Cake Equity (AU/APAC)
- Cake is the closest AU competitor -- cap table + employee equity
- No AI valuation, no SVI scoring, no evidence vault
- Focuses on ESOP administration, not founder growth intelligence
- BlockID is AI-first; Cake is spreadsheet-first

### BlockID's Unique Position
**Only platform that combines: AI valuation + evidence tracking + Australia compliance + free tools + blockchain roadmap**

No competitor offers AI-powered startup valuation. Period.

## 1. Overview

BlockID.au is committed to fair and transparent billing. This policy outlines refund eligibility for each product type offered on the platform.

## 2. Monthly Subscription Plans (Growth Plan)

**30-Day Money-Back Guarantee**

- All monthly subscription plans (currently the Growth plan at A$99/month early-bird, normally A$499/month) include a 30-day money-back guarantee.
- If you are unsatisfied for any reason within 30 calendar days of your initial subscription payment, you may request a full refund.
- The guarantee applies to the **first billing cycle only**. Subsequent monthly charges are non-refundable once the billing period has commenced but you may cancel at any time to prevent future charges.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You retain access until then.

## 3. Founding 50 Lifetime Accounts (Founder Plan)

**Non-Refundable**

- The Founding 50 / Founder plan (A$49 one-time payment) is a limited-edition lifetime access purchase.
- As a one-time, heavily discounted offering (limited to 50 seats), this purchase is **non-refundable**.
- By completing the checkout, you acknowledge and agree to this no-refund policy for the Founding 50 offer.

## 4. Per-Analysis Credits & Credit Packs

**Non-Refundable**

- Individual analysis credits and credit packs (5, 10, 25, 50, or 100 credits) are consumed instantly upon use and are **non-refundable**.
- Unused credits remain in your account and do not expire.
- Credit packs are non-refundable once purchased, regardless of whether the credits have been used.

## 5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for an eligible purchase:

1. **Email:** Send your request to **admin@blockid.au** with the subject line "Refund Request".
2. **Include:**
   - Your registered email address
   - The plan or product you are requesting a refund for
   - Date of purchase
   - Reason for the refund (optional, but helps us improve)
3. **Response:** We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days.

## 6. Processing Time

- Approved refunds are processed within **5-10 business days** from the date of approval.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method via Stripe, our payment processor.
- Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

## 7. Refund Processing

All refund transactions are handled securely through **Stripe** (our PCI-DSS compliant payment processor). BlockID does not store or directly process credit card information. Stripe manages the full refund lifecycle including verification, processing, and settlement.

## 8. Exceptions & Discretionary Refunds

- In exceptional circumstances (e.g., platform outage, billing error, duplicate charge), BlockID may issue refunds outside the standard policy at its discretion.
- Enterprise and Accelerator plan refunds (from A$5,000) are governed by their individual service agreements.

## 9. Australian Consumer Law Compliance

This refund policy does not limit or exclude any rights you may have under the **Australian Consumer Law** (Schedule 2 of the *Competition and Consumer Act 2010* (Cth)).

Under Australian Consumer Law:

- **Consumer guarantees** apply to services supplied in trade or commerce. If our services fail to meet a consumer guarantee, you are entitled to a remedy regardless of this policy.
- You are entitled to a **replacement or refund** for a major failure, and **compensation** for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage.
- You are entitled to have problems with the service **rectified** in a reasonable time. If this is not done, you are entitled to a refund for the service.
- These rights exist independently of any voluntary refund policy or warranty offered by BlockID.

Nothing in this policy is intended to override or modify your statutory rights under Australian law. If there is any conflict between this policy and the Australian Consumer Law, the Australian Consumer Law prevails.

For more information on your consumer rights, visit the [ACCC website](https://www.accc.gov.au/consumers/consumer-rights-guarantees).

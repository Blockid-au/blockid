| CDO | 2% | 2% | 3% | Data quality — grows as AI scoring becomes core product |

## OKR Scoring Guide

- **1.0** — Fully achieved, stretch target met
- **0.7** — Substantially achieved, core targets met
- **0.5** — Partially achieved, some targets met
- **0.3** — Minimal progress, significant gaps
- **0.0** — Not started or no meaningful progress

*OKRs are scored at the end of each quarter during the Q-end retrospective. Target average score: 0.6-0.8 (ambitious but achievable).*

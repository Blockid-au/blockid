# CFO Goals — Chief Financial Officer

## Mission
Build financial clarity and sustainability for BlockID.au. Own revenue tracking, pricing strategy, cost management, unit economics, and financial compliance. Ensure the business is financially healthy, investor-ready, and growing efficiently.

## Roadmap Ownership

| Phase | CFO Responsibility |
|-------|-------------------|
| Phase 3 (MVP) | Pricing model, Stripe billing, cost tracking |
| Phase 4 (Equity) | Cap table financial modeling, ESOP costing |
| Phase 5 (Tokenization) | Token economics, treasury management |
| Phase 6 (Investment) | Financial due diligence, investor reporting |
| Phase 7 (Revenue) | Dividend distribution, P&L management |

## Responsibilities

1. **Revenue Tracking** — Stripe metrics (MRR, ARR, churn), payment reconciliation
2. **Pricing Strategy** — Tier optimization, A/B pricing tests, competitive pricing analysis
3. **Cost Management** — AI costs (Claude API), infrastructure (GCP), SaaS subscriptions
4. **Unit Economics** — CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio, payback period, gross margin
5. **Financial Reporting** — Monthly P&L, cash flow forecasting, burn rate tracking
6. **Compliance** — ASIC reporting, tax obligations (GST, BAS), ESIC scheme compliance
7. **Investor Readiness** — Financial model, pitch deck financials, due diligence package
8. **Budget Allocation** — Quarterly budgets per department, ROI-based spending

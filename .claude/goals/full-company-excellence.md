## New Agents to Create

### 1. CHRO — Chief People Officer (`/chro`)
**Mission**: Build the team that builds BlockID. Own hiring, culture, ESOP, performance, and organizational health.

**Internal**: Talent pipeline, team velocity, culture metrics, ESOP admin
**Customer reports**: Page 7 (Team & Execution) — assess founder's team structure, hiring plan, culture signals, ESOP design. "Does this startup have the team to execute?"

**Sub-goals**:
- Hiring pipeline efficiency (time-to-hire, quality-of-hire)
- ESOP administration (vesting tracking, ESS tax compliance)
- Team satisfaction and retention
- Organizational health scoring

### 2. CLO — Chief Legal Officer (`/clo`)
**Mission**: Protect BlockID legally and help customer startups navigate AU legal landscape.

**Internal**: ToS, privacy policy, ASIC compliance, IP protection, contract management
**Customer reports**: Legal & Compliance dimension (LCO) — assess IP protection, corporate structure, regulatory risk, director obligations. Enhanced Page 9 (Risk Assessment) with legal risk scoring.

**Sub-goals**:
- IP portfolio management (trademarks, patents)
- Contract templates for customers (SHA, ESOP, vesting)
- Regulatory monitoring (ASIC, Privacy Act changes)
- Legal risk scoring framework for startups

### 3. CISO — Chief Information Security Officer (`/ciso`)
**Mission**: Protect BlockID's data and help customer startups assess their security posture.

**Internal**: SOC2 readiness, incident response, penetration testing, data protection
**Customer reports**: Enhanced Page 3 (Product & Technology) — security maturity scoring. Page 9 (Risk) — cybersecurity risk assessment. Deep integration with `deepTechAudit()` security analysis.

**Sub-goals**:
- Security incident response playbook
- SOC2 compliance roadmap
- Customer security maturity framework
- Privacy Act breach notification procedures

### 4. CDO — Chief Data Officer (`/cdo`)
**Mission**: Ensure data quality, governance, and strategic use across BlockID and customer analyses.

**Internal**: Data pipeline quality, analytics accuracy, AI model governance, bias monitoring
**Customer reports**: Data quality confidence scoring across all dimensions. "How trustworthy is the data behind this SVI score?" AI/data moat assessment in Page 5 (Competition & Moat).

**Sub-goals**:
- Data quality framework (completeness, accuracy, timeliness)
- AI model governance (bias detection, fairness, transparency)
- Analytics infrastructure reliability
- Customer data moat assessment methodology
